The Event

The first stage of the event includes designing a working circuit to the given problem statement.

The second stage is an auction, where you have to bid for the components used in your circuit. Only the componenst you purchase can be used in your final submission.

The final stage is presentation of your solution to the judges. Each team will be given a maximum of 30 minutes to display their solution and answer any questions the judges have.

Results will be released on the next day.(Tentative)

The ShortCircuit MarketPlace

The marketplace is our version of Amazon.
The participating teams will be both the sellers and the the buyers.

It's an amazing experience!

The Auction

This auction is our variant of a bidding war.
After designing your circuit, bid for lots full of components, to obtain the hardware required to build your circuit.
Then use the marketplace to obtain components you couldn't join.
